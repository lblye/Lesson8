What Have I learned?

May 21, 2013

Yikes! That's a loaded question! I've learned (and delighted in this) that logic and organization are just as important as creativity and style when building a website. For me, it has been more important to focus on grasping the fundamentals so that my code is clean and my stylesheet makes sense. Pretty will have to come later.

I've also learned that when a spot becomes available for you overnight in a class you desperately need to take but are woefully unprepared for, know that you will feel a little out of sync for a long time, and that you will have to celebrate the things you CAN do and not dwell on the things you are still trying to learn. Today I can build a website. Might not be pretty or robust, but before April 1st, 2013, I could not. And that's worth celebrating!

For sure I've learned that I'm a much better singer than I am a coder!
